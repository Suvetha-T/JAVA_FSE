Community News

New Community Center Opens

After two years of construction, our new state-of-the-art community center is finally open to the public. The facility features a gymnasium, swimming pool, library, and multiple meeting rooms available for community use.

Mayor Johnson cut the ribbon at yesterday's ceremony, stating, "This center represents our commitment to bringing neighbors together and fostering community spirit." The center will host its first public event this Saturday - a family fun day with activities for all ages.

Upcoming Road Works

Beginning next Monday, Main Street will undergo resurfacing work that may cause traffic delays. The project is expected to last three weeks, with work occurring weekdays between 9am and 3pm.

Alternative routes will be signposted, and access to all businesses will be maintained throughout the project. The city council apologizes for any inconvenience and thanks residents for their patience during these necessary improvements.

Annual Food Drive Success

Last weekend's food drive collected over 5,000 pounds of non-perishable items for the local food bank, smashing last year's record of 3,800 pounds. Organizers credit the success to increased participation from local schools and businesses.
